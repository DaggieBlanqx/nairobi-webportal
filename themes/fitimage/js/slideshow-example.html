New Crude Oil Transmission Technology

AOT™ Midstream and AOT™ Upstream

Applied Oil Technology (AOT™) improves the flow of industrial crude oil down pipelines via a unique and revolutionary form of viscosity reduction. AOT™ is on track to be implemented in the North American market.

AOT™ Installation

AOT™ is installed at pipeline pump stations, to reduce the viscosity of crude oil flow through pipelines. The devices are housed in standard pressure vessels rated to the system pressure rating.
